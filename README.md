# Customer-Engagement-Retention-PowerBI
Customer Engagement &amp; Retention Analytics Reports . This project analyzes bank customers churn using Power BI by focusing on customer engagement, product utilization, and relationship strength.

**Objective:**

To analyze customer churn using engagement and product utilization metrics and identify retention risk segments.
Dataset Description:
The dataset contains customer demographic, engagement, financial, and product usage information.

**Dashboard Description:**

 The Power BI dashboard consists of three pages:
 
Churn & Retention Overview


Engagement & Product Analysis


Retention Risk & Strategy


**Key Insights:**
Inactive Customers Shows Higher Churn.
Single Product Customers are more likely to Churn.
High Balance Inactive Customers present Silent Churn Risk. 

 Methodology:
   1.  Customers are divided into active and inactive to understand engagement.

       
   2.  Customer churn is compared based on how many bank products they use.


   3.  Churn is analyzed for customers with and without credit cards.


Customers are grouped into strong, medium, and weak relationships based on engagement and product usage.
High-balance but inactive customers are identified to find hidden churn risk.


** Recommendations:**

Banks should focus more on keeping customers engaged rather than only giving discounts.


Customers using only one product should be encouraged to use more bank products.


High-value customers who are inactive should be contacted early to prevent churn.


**Conclusion:**

Customers stay with the bank mainly because they are engaged and use multiple products, not just because they have high balance or salary.
